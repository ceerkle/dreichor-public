# ADR-003 — Trust Lifecycle & Escalation Rules

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Why trust escalation must be explicit and governed.

## Decision
Definition of trust states and allowed transitions.

## Trust Levels
OBSERVED → CONSTRAINED → TRUSTED → EXTENDED

## Allowed Transitions
- what is allowed
- what is forbidden

## Escalation Rules
- explicit conditions
- no implicit promotion
- no time-based shortcuts

## Consequences
- determinism
- auditability
- licensing implications

## Non-Goals
- optimization
- learning
- automatic autonomy
